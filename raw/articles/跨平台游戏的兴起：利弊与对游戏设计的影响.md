Not long ago, playing a game with friends across different devices seemed impossible. Systems like PlayStation, Xbox, PC, and mobile were isolated worlds. Players could only connect with others using the same platform.  
不久前，和朋友们在不同设备上玩游戏似乎几乎不可能。像 PlayStation、Xbox、PC 和移动端这样的系统是孤立的世界。玩家只能使用同一平台与其他玩家连接。

That’s no longer the case.  
现在情况已经不一样了。

Games like Fortnite, Call of Duty: Warzone, and Rocket League changed expectations across the industry. This article explores how crossplay is reshaping gaming—from the benefits and tradeoffs to the impact on game design for both players and developers.  
像《堡垒之夜》、《使命召唤：战区》和《火箭联盟》这样的游戏改变了整个行业的期待。本文探讨跨平台游戏如何重塑游戏——从利弊到对玩家和开发者游戏设计的影响。

## The Evolution of Cross-Play in Gaming跨平台游戏在游戏中的演变

For years, gaming platforms were closed ecosystems. PlayStation users couldn’t connect with Xbox players. PC gamers were separated entirely. Technical limitations, competitive interests, and different network systems built thick walls between communities.  
多年来，游戏平台一直是封闭生态系统。PlayStation 用户无法与 Xbox 玩家连接。PC 玩家则完全被隔离开来。技术限制、竞争利益和不同的网络系统在社区之间筑起了厚重的墙壁。

In the early 2000s, multiplayer games were mostly restricted to a single console network. Xbox Live and PlayStation Network kept players locked into their respective environments. Developers who tried to bridge those gaps often hit roadblocks from platform policies or tech limitations.  
在 2000 年代初，多人游戏大多局限于单一主机网络。Xbox Live 和 PlayStation Network 让玩家被锁定在各自的环境中。试图弥合这些差距的开发者常常遇到平台政策或技术限制的障碍。

### Pressure From Players and Publishers来自玩家和发行商的压力

As online gaming exploded, players began asking: Why can’t I play with friends on other devices? Publishers noticed too. A bigger, unified player base keeps online communities alive longer, strengthens matchmaking, and helps multiplayer games stay relevant.  
随着网络游戏的爆发，玩家们开始质疑：为什么我不能和朋友在其他设备上一起玩？出版商也注意到了。更大且统一的玩家基础能延长线上社区的活力，增强匹配机制，并帮助多人游戏保持相关性。

Gradually, the industry started paying attention. Several key games pushed crossplay forward:  
渐渐地，行业开始关注这一趋势。几款关键游戏推动了跨平台的进步：

- Minecraft: Bedrock Edition unified Xbox, PlayStation, PC, and mobile into shared worlds.  
	《我的世界：基岩版》将 Xbox、PlayStation、PC 和移动端统一成共享世界。
- Rocket League showed that even competitive games could handle mixed-platform play without breaking the experience.  
	《火箭联盟》证明了即使是竞技游戏也能在不同平台玩法下不破坏体验。
- Fortnite elevated crossplay to a mainstream expectation, proving players want it—and that it works at scale.  
	《堡垒之夜》将跨平台提升到了主流期待，证明玩家们渴望它——而且它能大规模运行。

These successes opened the door for more studios to embrace cross-platform compatibility. Today, it’s almost standard for new multiplayer releases to support it—or face criticism if they don’t.  
这些成功为更多工作室接受跨平台兼容性打开了大门。如今，新多人游戏几乎默认支持此功能——否则如果不支持就会受到批评。

## The Advantages of Cross-Play跨平台的优势

Cross-platform play brings real benefits for both players and developers.  
跨平台游戏为玩家和开发者带来了真正的好处。

### Larger Player Bases 更大的玩家基础

The most obvious upside is a unified community. Instead of splitting users by device, all players enter the same pool. This leads to faster matchmaking, better skill balancing, and healthier multiplayer ecosystems.  
最明显的好处是社区的统一。所有玩家不再按设备分开用户，而是进入同一个池。这带来了更快的匹配、更好的技能平衡以及更健康的多人游戏生态系统。

In smaller games, player counts per platform might be too low for good matchmaking. But combined across devices, the pool grows, creating a more balanced and enjoyable experience.  
在小型游戏中，每个平台的玩家数量可能太少，无法做好匹配。但跨设备组合后，泳池会不断扩大，带来更平衡、更愉快的体验。

[![](https://i0.wp.com/twistedvoxel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/marvel-snap-scaled.jpg?w=2560&ssl=1)](https://i0.wp.com/twistedvoxel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/marvel-snap-scaled.jpg?ssl=1)

### Freedom of Choice 选择自由

Gamers no longer need to buy the same hardware as their friends. Whether using a PlayStation, Xbox, PC, or mobile phone, people can play together. That freedom makes multiplayer gaming more inclusive and flexible.  
玩家们不再需要和朋友购买相同的硬件。无论是使用 PlayStation、Xbox、PC 还是手机，人们都可以一起玩。这种自由让多人游戏更加包容和灵活。

It also allows players to use their preferred device—whether it’s a gaming rig, living room console, or smartphone—without worrying about compatibility.  
它还允许玩家使用自己喜欢的设备——无论是游戏设备、客厅主机还是智能手机——无需担心兼容性问题。

### Longer Game Lifespans 更长的游戏寿命

For developers, crossplay keeps communities alive longer. Instead of watching your user base shrink as people move to new platforms or consoles, a connected system allows continued growth.  
对开发者来说，跨平台让社区存续更久。与其眼睁睁看着用户群随着用户迁移到新平台或主机而缩小，联网系统促进了持续增长。

A unified player base supports features like cosmetics, in-game events, and microtransactions—all of which depend on consistent engagement. With more players active for longer, long-term monetization becomes more sustainable.  
统一的玩家基础支持外观、游戏内活动和微交易等功能——这些都依赖于持续的参与度。随着更多玩家持续活跃，长期变现变得更加可持续。

## The Challenges for Developers开发者的挑战

But the benefits come with a cost. Cross-platform play introduces several technical and design challenges.  
但好处是伴随着代价的。跨平台游戏带来了若干技术和设计挑战。

### Balancing Gameplay 平衡玩法

Different input methods create uneven advantages. For example, PC players using a mouse and keyboard may have faster aim compared to controller users.  
不同的输入方式会产生不均衡的优势。例如，使用鼠标和键盘的 PC 玩家可能比手柄玩家瞄准更快。

Developers must carefully manage these imbalances. Common solutions include:  
开发者必须谨慎管理这些不平衡。常见的解决方案包括：

- Grouping players by input type during matchmaking  
	匹配过程中按输入类型分组玩家
- Adjusting aim assist for controller players  
	为手柄玩家调整瞄准辅助
- Allowing users to opt out of crossplay if desired  
	允许用户选择退出跨平台游戏

Striking the right balance without limiting the experience is a delicate task.  
在不限制体验的前提下找到恰当的平衡是一项微妙的任务。

### Cheating and Moderation 作弊与节制

PC platforms are more vulnerable to cheating and exploits than consoles. With crossplay, console users may worry about facing unfair advantages when matched with PC players.  
PC 平台比主机更容易被作弊和利用。通过跨平台联机，主机用户可能会担心与 PC 玩家匹配时会面临不公平的优势。

Developers need robust anti-cheat systems and moderation tools that work across all platforms. Since each system (Xbox, PlayStation, PC) has different requirements, managing this adds complexity to development and support.  
开发者需要强大的反作弊系统和跨平台的管理工具。由于每个系统（Xbox、PlayStation、PC）的需求不同，管理这些问题会增加开发和支持的复杂性。

### Monetization and Store Policies变现与商店政策

Each platform has its own payment systems, rules, and store restrictions. Crossplay complicates in-game purchases, especially when items are expected to carry over between platforms.  
每个平台都有自己的支付系统、规则和店铺限制。跨平台联机使游戏内购买变得复杂，尤其是当物品预计会在不同平台之间转移时。

For example, a player who buys a cosmetic on Xbox expects to see it available on their PC. Developers must work with each platform provider (e.g., Sony, Microsoft, Apple) to ensure purchases remain valid and synced—without violating platform agreements.  
例如，一个玩家在 Xbox 上购买了一个装饰品，期望它能在自己的 PC 上看到。开发者必须与每个平台提供商（如索尼、Microsoft、苹果）合作，确保购买保持有效且同步——不违反平台协议。

## Cross-Play Beyond Gaming – Lessons From Other Industries跨平台游戏——来自其他行业的经验教训

Cross-platform connection isn’t just a gaming trend—it reflects a broader shift across the digital world toward seamless integration between ecosystems.  
跨平台连接不仅仅是游戏的趋势——它反映了数字世界中向生态系统无缝整合的更广泛转变。

### Managing Communities Across Platforms跨平台管理社区

Just like game developers, companies and creators also face challenges managing communities across different digital spaces. Social media platforms, websites, apps—they all fragment communication.  
就像游戏开发者一样，公司和创作者在管理不同数字空间的社区时也面临挑战。社交媒体平台、网站、应用程序——它们都分散了沟通。

An example is creator-focused tools that help unify engagement. Platforms like [OnlyMonster](https://onlymonster.ai/) give users a central space to manage content, track performance, and connect with audiences across ecosystems. It mirrors how crossplay connects players—by removing friction between platforms and providing a more unified experience.  
例如，以创作者为中心的工具有助于统一互动。像 [OnlyMonster](https://onlymonster.ai/) 这样的平台为用户提供了一个集中管理内容、跟踪表现以及跨生态系统受众连接的空间。它反映了跨平台连接玩家的方式——消除平台间的摩擦，提供更统一的体验。

Whether in games or business, creating shared experiences across systems requires thoughtful structure. Without it, users feel disconnected or left out.  
无论是在游戏还是商业领域，跨系统创建共享体验都需要深思熟虑的结构。没有它，用户会感到疏离或被排除在外。

In games, crossplay creates a seamless world for players. In digital business, dedicated platforms offer the infrastructure that lets creators bring their communities together. Both rely on smart system design to deliver consistency and reliability—regardless of device or location.  
在游戏中，跨平台为玩家创造了一个无缝的世界。在数字商业中，专门平台为创作者提供了将社区凝聚在一起的基础设施。两者都依赖智能系统设计来实现一致性和可靠性——无论设备或地点如何。

## The Future of Cross-Play 跨平台的未来

The shift toward cross-platform play is still unfolding—and its influence on game design is just beginning.  
向跨平台游玩的转变仍在进行中——其对游戏设计的影响才刚刚开始。

- **A New Industry Standard.** Today, players expect crossplay. It’s no longer a bonus—it’s the default. Games that don’t offer it are often criticized or seen as outdated. Developers now face pressure to make cross-platform support part of their launch plans.  
	**新的行业标准。** 如今，玩家期待跨平台对战。这不再是额外奖励——而是默认的。没有这些功能的游戏常常被批评或被视为过时。开发者现在面临将跨平台支持纳入其发布计划的压力。
- **Cross-Platform Economies.** One major area of growth is shared economies. In-game purchases and progression need to follow players from one platform to another. Games like Fortnite and Warzone already do this, allowing players to keep their skins, levels, and rewards no matter where they log in. Expect more titles to adopt these models—giving players real ownership of their accounts and purchases.  
	**跨平台经济。** 一个主要的增长领域是共享经济。游戏内购买和进度需要跟随玩家从一个平台到另一个平台。像《堡垒之夜》和《战区》这样的游戏已经做到了这一点，允许玩家无论在哪里登录，都能保留自己的皮肤、等级和奖励。预计会有更多游戏采用这些模式——让玩家真正拥有自己的账户和购买。
- **Cloud Gaming and the End of Hardware Divisions.** Streaming services like Xbox Cloud Gaming and NVIDIA GeForce Now could push this trend even further. If games are streamed from the cloud, the device used becomes less relevant. In that future, it won’t just be about connecting consoles and PCs—it’ll be about removing hardware barriers altogether. Players will simply log in, no matter where they are, and connect instantly with others.  
	**云游戏与硬件分裂的终结。** 像 Xbox 云游戏和 NVIDIA GeForce Now 这样的流媒体服务可能会进一步推动这一趋势。如果游戏是从云端流媒体传输的，所用设备的重要性就会降低。在那个未来，不仅仅是连接主机和个人电脑——更是彻底消除硬件障碍。无论玩家身处何地，都能直接登录，并立即与其他人连接。

## Conclusion 结论

Cross-platform play has evolved from a dream into an industry requirement. What started with a few experimental titles is now shaping the future of multiplayer gaming.  
跨平台游戏已经从梦想发展成为行业必需。最初只有几款实验性游戏，如今正塑造着多人游戏的未来。

Its benefits are clear: larger communities, better matchmaking, longer game lifespans. But it also brings challenges in balance, cheating, and monetization. Developers who can solve these issues will create more connected, loyal, and long-lasting player bases.  
它的好处显而易见：更大的社区、更好的匹配系统、更长的游戏寿命。但这也带来了平衡性、作弊和变现的挑战。能够解决这些问题的开发者将创造更多连接、忠诚且持久的玩家群。

Just as specialized digital tools help creators manage audiences across platforms, crossplay lets developers do the same for gamers. Both point toward a future where barriers are removed—and shared experiences take center stage.  
正如专门的数字工具帮助创作者跨平台管理受众一样，跨平台游戏也让开发者能够为玩家做到同样的事。两者都指向一个障碍被消除、共享体验成为焦点的未来。
